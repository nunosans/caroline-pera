After finishing my foundation studies in Fine Art at Central Saint Martins College of Art & Design I progressed to the BA (Hons) Jewellery Design from which I have recently graduated.

Hi, I'm Caroline. I am a 23-year-old Jewellery Designer born in Milan, Italy. This webpage is a collection of the work I have done over the past few years. While thinking and working on new pieces for my collection I do a lot of painting and develop my interests in Photography and Fine Arts.

In 2008 I have won the Hoarward Hobbs award for Jewellery prospectives and back in 2006 The British Art Medal Society award. My work was exhibited in the EC1 exhibition in 2007 and in 2006 I have won the Swarovski competition. I have done work experience as an apprentice at Gem Palace in India in 2006 and last year. Recently I have been an intern at Verdura and Bulgari in New York.
